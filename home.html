INSECURE & DANGEREUX - UPDATE 2021

The show must go on! Insecure and Dangereux continues in January, with a smaller capacity and our anti-Covid procedures. We will usually perform two shows on a night to accommodate more audience. Most shows are now 75 minutes with no intermission. Bad news: Due to Corona restrictions, our bar is temporarily closed. Good news: You are allowed to bring in (store bought) drinks! We will provide water, glasses, ice, and coolers and will also clean things up afterwards. This is as close as you will get to a bar over the next few weeks! You are allowed to enter our venue until 20:00 with your drinks. We are charging a ‘good news surcharge’ of five euros per person to help offset the losses of lower capacities during these restrictions. Thank you in advance for your support of the arts. Book with confidence! If you ever book a show that has to be cancelled, we will always reluctantly refund your money with a forced smile.
